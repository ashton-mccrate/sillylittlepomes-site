privacy

how Silly Little Pomes handles your data.

Silly Little Pomes collects anonymous usage data through TelemetryDeck so we can see which features people use. It's limited to an anonymized per-install identifier, your app version and device type, and simple event names like "poem saved." This never includes the content of your writing, and it can't be traced back to you. You can read how TelemetryDeck handles data in their privacy policy.

Silly Little Pomes is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under 13.
